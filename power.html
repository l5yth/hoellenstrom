Generator Situation

ON-SITE: 40kVA Backup Generator (Generator Garage)

Connected via NAYY 4×50, 50m, 80A to guest house with manual transfer switch. Either grid or generator feeds the guest house side — not both. Direct outputs: CEE 32A + 2×CEE 16A + Schuko 16A.

Normal operation: grid feeds guest house, generator on standby backup. Standalone mode: generator outputs used directly for hacker tent, guest house continues on grid via transfer switch set to grid side. Valid approach if E.DIS supply is reliable.

RENTAL: ~50kVA Silent — Camper Van Parking

Required for 25-van hookup area. Position at camper parking near hotel. Feed a 25-slot CEE 16A distribution board (one RCD per van). Silent canopy — overnight operation near sleeping guests. 48h+ fuel tank preferred for unattended overnight running.

✓ Only one rental generator likely needed. With talks/workshop loads on both stages and the on-site 40kVA gen handling the hacker tent, the only remaining gap is the van parking area. This is a significant reduction from earlier estimates.
⚠ If hacker tent uses on-site gen (Option B): guest house side loses its backup coverage during that time. A grid outage on the guest house feed takes down beer barn, pool area, main stage, food garden, and hotel simultaneously with no fallback. Assess E.DIS reliability for the area or negotiate a second manual transfer point with site owner.
