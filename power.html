Program context: talks, workshops, hacking. No festival PA, no moving light rigs.

Generator Situation

ON-SITE: 40kVA Backup Generator (Generator Garage, near Gasthaus)

Normal: manual transfer on grid side. Generator on hot standby for Gasthaus hub. Outputs (CEE 32A + 2×CEE 16A + Schuko 16A) are live when running.

Standalone mode: transfer switch set to grid for Gasthaus, generator outputs used independently for hacker tent (~150m cable from gen garage). Guest house side continues on grid. Frees up the Gasthaus 32A circuit for other use (e.g. forest locations).

RENTAL: ~50kVA Silent — RV/Camper Parking

Position at or between the two camper parking areas. 25-slot CEE 16A distribution board. Silent canopy mandatory — overnight operation near sleeping guests. 48h+ diesel tank preferred.

✓ Minimum rental: one 50kVA unit for camper parking. Everything else runs on grid. If hacker tent uses the on-site gen standalone, no additional rental is needed at all for the program areas.
