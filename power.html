Key assumptions — All 32A connections assumed 3-phase CEE (Drehstrom 400V): 32A×400V×√3 ≈ 22kW max, ~18kW usable (cos φ 0.8). 16A assumed single-phase 230V: ≈ 3.7kW. 63A assumed 3-phase: ≈ 44kW. If any 32A connections are single-phase only (230V), capacity drops to ~7.4kW per circuit — verify with site owner. Diversity factors applied to camping loads (0.2–0.3). Stage/PA loads taken at 80% of rated.

Generator Requirements

GEN-01 · Main Stage · 100kVA silent

Location: adjacent to infrastructure house. Feeds PA rig and lighting. Utility infra-house circuits handle everything else. Diesel, silent canopy, 48h+ fuel tank preferred. Position upwind of stage or in dedicated enclosure.

GEN-02 · Circus Tent · 100kVA silent

Location: behind or beside hotel/circus tent. Full PA and lighting load. Same spec as GEN-01. Both generators can be sourced from the same rental — negotiate a two-unit deal. Run 12h minimum per night.

GEN-03 · Camper Van Parking · 50kVA

Location: camper parking area near hotel. Feeds hookup distribution board for 25 vans. Silent canopy, 16A CEE outlets on distribution board, one RCD per van. Can potentially be shared with circus tent via cable if proximity allows — but noise and fuel logistics separate is cleaner.

Optional GEN-04: If electric shower count exceeds 6 units simultaneously, a 30kVA unit feeding the shower block as supplement to the pink house 63A circuit gives headroom. Assess once shower vendor confirms unit count.
